About Vinyl Fantasy

This forum was actually inspired by a real-life favorite hangout of mine, Bushwick-based comic book and vinyl store Vinyl Fantasy. It was meant for the owners to post about new releases, events and other miscellaneous news about their store.

This was created before I even knew about the joy of AngularJS, but it was a joy to create this and challenge myself.
