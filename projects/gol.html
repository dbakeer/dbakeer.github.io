About GOL

As one of our projects for General Assembly, we were tasked with creating a Rails/Angular app. I didn't want to do a simple To-Do app, but I also wanted to get real-life practice with Rails. I also grew incredibly comfortable with Rails and loved integrating the relational database.

I have many goals in my life, but I love visualizing and writing them down, including the steps it would take to reach those goals. Each goal category is separated and color-coded to encourage user organization. Additionally, I got to flex one of my biggest loves in coding--styling with AngularJS.
