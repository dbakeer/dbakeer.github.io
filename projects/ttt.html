About Tic Tac Totoro

This is the first app I ever made with Javascript, and I include it in my portfolio because it brings me a lot of pride and happiness to see how far I have come. It's a basic Javascript tic-tac-toe game, one that almost every rookie developer makes. Despite that, it was a joy to work on it.

Another reason I include it in my website is because it was styled in a way that hearkens back to my old-school Expages and Geocities designs as a kid. I used to use Paint Shop Pro and design layouts for Sailor Moon and Final Fantasy fansites--web design was a hobby for me before it became a profession. Even if it's a simple Javascript game, looking at it brings a smile to my face.
