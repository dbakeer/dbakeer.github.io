About Kawaii Dreamer

At the time this project was made, we had experienced the devastating terrorist attacks in Paris and the ongoing turmoil in the Middle East, Syria in particular. I wanted to make something that showed what united us--not what divided us. Kawaii Dreamer may seem cutesy, but it comes from a place of sadness and hope. What is your dream? How do you want to change this world and your own life?

This was a straightforward single-page app that utilized the flexible Google Maps API and simple styling with Angular.
