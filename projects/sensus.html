About Sensus

It was an incredible experience to work with Shuli and Mae to create this project. Shuli cultivated the incredible idea to create a mood visualization app that would show the user the fluctuations in their moods. From there, we all set out to create this app.

This was an enjoyable experience, especially with expanding my personal love and interest in AngularJS and the flexibility that Rails offers. Moreover, I loved working with Shuli and Mae and hope to do so more in the future.
