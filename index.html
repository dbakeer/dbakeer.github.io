About

Dinah Bakeer was born in New Orleans, Louisiana. She spent her formative years making websites on a dubious Dial-Up connection. As technology progressed and she explored the world, she eventually found that web development can be a creative field, and since then pursued it relentlessly. She moonlights as a writer (published in Missbehave Magazine and Thrillist), painted faces on movie sets and editorial in New Orleans, and retired her wrestling boots. You may now find her trying to finish Seiken Densetsu 3.
